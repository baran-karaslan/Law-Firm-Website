

<p>The scope of this field covers the legal support required by commercial enterprises from their establishment to their daily operations, growth processes, and the resolution of potential disputes. Matters such as selecting the appropriate company type, drafting articles of association, ensuring the legality of board resolutions, conducting general assembly procedures, and assessing the rights and obligations of shareholders are handled with precision. In addition, preparing, revising, and negotiating commercial contracts is essential for minimizing legal risks. Support is provided in resolving disputes arising from commercial operations, including reputation issues, receivables, competition-related matters, and application-based conflicts. The primary objective is to help businesses operate safely, sustainably, and in full compliance with the law, thereby minimizing risks arising from commercial activities.</p>
