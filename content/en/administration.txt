

<p>Public administration decisions can be unilateral and may lead to serious consequences. Therefore, detailed legal evaluations are conducted regarding administrative fines, license cancellations, civil servant disciplinary actions, public tenders, expropriation, and other administrative procedures. Administrative applications, objection procedures, and lawsuits before administrative courts are carefully managed to ensure the legality of administrative actions and protect individual rights. Preventive legal consultancy is also offered to help individuals and companies navigate administrative processes more effectively.</p>
