

<p>Real estate transactions carry significant financial risks and therefore require careful legal evaluation. Matters such as title transfers, sales, mortgages, usufruct rights, pre-emption rights, lease relations, condominium ownership, and partition proceedings are meticulously handled. Legal representation is provided in disputes involving property rights violations, zoning conflicts, expropriation processes, boundary disputes, and title cancellation and registration cases. Additional services include contract preparation, project consultancy, pre-sales procedures, and resolving disputes between contractors and property owners in construction projects. The primary aim is to ensure that real estate transactions are conducted lawfully and securely.</p>
