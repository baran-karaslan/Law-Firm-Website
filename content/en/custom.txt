

<p>Import and export procedures involve administrative processes such as declarations, inspections, valuation assessments, and customs duties. Legal actions are taken against administrative decisions, including additional assessments, penalties, confiscation, and liquidation of goods. Due to the technical nature of customs regulations, expert support is often essential in disputes. Detailed analysis is conducted on issues such as determining the origin, classification, valuation of goods, and entry into free circulation. Legal procedures are pursued to protect the rights and interests of clients facing allegations of non-compliance or administrative sanctions. The objective is to ensure that foreign trade activities proceed safely, swiftly, and in compliance with relevant regulations.</p>
