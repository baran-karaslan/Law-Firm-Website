

<p>Debt collection and restructuring processes require careful management due to the technical structure of enforcement and bankruptcy law. Professional assistance is provided in general execution proceedings, proceedings based on court judgments, negotiable instruments, seizure procedures, asset declarations, payment commitments, bankruptcy filings, and concordat processes.Cases involving objections to execution, lifting of objections, third-party claims, return of property (restitution) and protection of seized assets are handled meticulously. The goal is to ensure that enforcement procedures are completed efficiently, swiftly, and in full compliance with legal requirements.</p>
