

<p>Due to the dynamic and multi-stakeholder nature of the tourism sector, it is governed by various administrative and private law regulations. Comprehensive legal consultancy is provided for sector-specific institutions such as accommodation facilities, travel agencies, tour operators, and businesses offering guiding services. Legal solutions are developed for consumer complaints arising from customer relations, package tour agreements, reservation disputes, cancellation and refund processes, and similar issues. Guidance is also offered regarding licensing, certification, and administrative supervision procedures involving tourism businesses. The aim is to ensure that tourism services continue in a seamless, safe, and legally compliant manner in accordance with national and international regulations.</p>
